About Us

Wild Rescues is a non-profit organization dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and release of injured and orphaned wildlife. Our team of passionate volunteers works tirelessly to provide medical care, nutrition, and a safe environment for animals in need.

Founded in 2005, Wild Rescues has successfully rehabilitated and released over 1,000 animals back into their natural habitats. We believe that every creature deserves a second chance at life, and we are committed to making a positive impact on wildlife conservation.
