Frequently Asked Questions

What types of animals do you rescue?

We rescue a wide variety of wildlife, including birds, mammals, reptiles, and amphibians. Our team is trained to handle different species and provide appropriate care.

How can I help with your rescue efforts?

You can help by volunteering your time, donating supplies or funds, or spreading awareness about our organization. Please visit our Partnership page for more information on how to get involved.

What should I do if I find an injured animal?

If you find an injured animal, please contact us immediately. Do not attempt to handle the animal yourself, as it may be dangerous or further injured. Our team will provide guidance on how to safely assist the animal until we can take over.
