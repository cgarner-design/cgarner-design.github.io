Welcome to Wild Rescue

Our mission is to rescue and rehabilitate injured wildlife.

Welcome to our website! We are dedicated to helping injured wildlife and providing them with the care they need to recover and return to their natural habitat.We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping injured wildlife.

Our staff provides around-the-clock care for wildlife in need. We also provide long-term care for wildlife that cannot be released back into their natural environment.
